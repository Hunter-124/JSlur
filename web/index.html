🤖 AI Browser Search (vision) — options

Enable AI Browser Search (vision) in the list above to drive a real browser to each board's normal search page and have your AI model read the listings straight off screenshots. It browses like a person instead of hitting scrape endpoints, so it isn't rate-limited or Cloudflare-blocked. Needs a vision-capable AI provider (Claude, Gemini, a GPT-4o-class model, or a local vision model like llava). Connected accounts (LinkedIn / ZipRecruiter) are replayed here too.

Connected accounts (optional — sign in for better / unblocked scraping)

Connecting opens a fresh browser window at the board. Sign in — or just clear any “are you human?” check — and the app captures that session automatically. No password is stored. The captured cookies are replayed on scrapes, which signs you in (LinkedIn) and gets past Cloudflare blocks (ZipRecruiter, SimplyHired, …).
